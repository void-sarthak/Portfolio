Ignition Evade

About the Game

Seishin is a little fire spirit, and the protagonist of the game. He was enjoying himself at how but suddenly the heavy rainfall started. Making matter worse, the nearby dam was overflown causing flood in the area. Searching for higher grounds, Seishin ran away from the flood and entered the caves. In the caves he found the mysterious portal that took him to unkown land. Now trapped in these lands, Seishin needs to escape and go back to his home.

Ignition Evade is a 2D platform based, where the player plays as Seishin, helping him to escape the unknown lands and go back to his home. The game is designed to be played on PC and is developed using Unity Engine. The game is designed to be played using keyboard and mouse.

How to Play

Rules

Download Guide

  • Main objective is to solve puzzle and clear the levels

  • Controls
  • Use arrow keys to move the player.
  • Use space bar to jump.
  • When in air(while jumping), the player can use space to use his abilities like dash and pound
  • Use tab to switch between the alternate self.
  • Press M on the keyboard to flip the maps.

  • The player needs to travels all the cities and complete the levels to escape the world.
  • A level is completed when both player and its alternate self reaches the end of the level.
  • Player can use the abilities of his alternate self to solve the puzzles.
  • Dash can be used by the alternate self only.
  • Pound is triggered by player and the alternate player gets a jump boost.
  • Flip map and switch the controls to solve the levels.

Download the zip file, and extract the game. After extracting, click on the .exe file to play the game.
