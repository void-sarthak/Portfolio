Eco Hex

About the Game

EcoHex is a turn-based strategy game, you and the computer compete to control a hexagonal board by placing pawns. The goal is to establish dominance by strategically positioning your pawns and blocking the opponent pawns. The game allows customization of difficulty level, board size, and choice of who goes first, enabling a tailored experience for both beginners and seasoned players

Download Guide

  • The game is played turn by turn where the player can choose who to play first, computer or player itself.
  • Each player can place a pawn on the board in their turn.
  • The pawn can be played by clicking on the empty cell in the board.
  • The player can choose the difficulty lovel of the AI.
  • The player can choose the size of the board.
  • The player need to alling 4 cells in a row to win the game.
  • If player alligns 3 pawns in a row, before allinging 4 pawns, the player loses.
  • Player can allign the pawns in any of direction on the hexagonal grid
  • Player can also block the opponent pawns by placing their own pawns in between the opponent pawns.

    • Download the zip file, and extract the game. After extracting, click on the .exe file to play the game.
